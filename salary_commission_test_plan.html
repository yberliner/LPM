2. Deduction Chain Formula

1. VAT = Gross − (Gross / 1.18) 2. CC = Gross × 2.5% (CreditCard only, else 0) 3. Net After Deductions = Gross − VAT − CC 4. Reserve = Net After Deductions × 10% 5. NET BASE = Net After Deductions − Reserve
